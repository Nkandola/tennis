Why I likw to play tennis

Tennis gets you outside, improves hand eye coordination, improves cardio fitness, and gives a chance to meet a lot of new people. Tennis is a great sport to keep your mind active and challenge yourself. It can also help you to develop a stronger will and a more competitive nature, which can help in other areas of life. Why do I love playing tennis so much?
    Primarily because it's challenging and fun. It's something you can do for your whole life. There's always something to learn or get better at. There are opportunities to play in a variety of age and gender tennis leagues
